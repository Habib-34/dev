Social Media App

Flutter • Firebase • Cloud Storage • Real-time Database

Project Overview

A modern social media platform built with Flutter, featuring photo and video sharing, real-time messaging, story features, and social interactions. Designed with a focus on user engagement and seamless content creation and consumption.

Key Features

  • Photo and video sharing with filters
  • Stories feature with 24-hour expiry
  • Real-time messaging and group chats
  • Follow/unfollow and friend system
  • Like, comment, and share interactions
  • Explore page with trending content
  • Privacy settings and content moderation
